Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information from TikTok Lead Ads for the purpose of integrating advertising leads into our internal CRM system.

Information We Collect

When users authorize our application through TikTok OAuth, we access lead form data generated through TikTok advertising campaigns. This may include user-submitted information such as name, email, phone number, and other form fields configured by the advertiser.

How We Use the Information

The collected data is used solely for CRM synchronization and internal lead management. We do not share or sell this information to any third parties.

Data Retention

Lead data is stored only as long as necessary for CRM operations. Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

Data Deletion Requests

To request deletion of your data, please contact us at: your-email@example.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
